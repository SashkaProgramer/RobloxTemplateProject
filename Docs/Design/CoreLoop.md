# Core Loop

## Loop Summary

Players spawn into a night train raid, move through connected wagons, gather loot and key resources, survive threats, decide who to trust, and attempt to extract before limited escape capacity runs out.

## Core Loop Formula

`enter raid -> move forward -> loot -> survive pressure -> distrust grows -> extract or lose everything`

## Emotional Arc

1. Curiosity
2. Tension
3. Reliance on others
4. Suspicion
5. Panic
6. Relief or frustration

## Core Decisions

- Do I stay with the group or split off?
- Do I carry utility items or chase high-value loot?
- Do I help open the path or let others take the risk?
- Do I sabotage now or wait until extraction?
- Do I protect rare loot or hide that I found it?
