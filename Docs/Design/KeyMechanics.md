# Key Mechanics

## MVP Mechanics

### Loot and Inventory

- Limited inventory slots
- Valuable run-based loot
- Rare loot with high social pressure

### Sabotage PvP

- Temporary door blocking
- Power disruption
- Noise generation to pull threats
- Theft or relocation of key resources

### PvE Pressure

- At least one main hostile threat
- Threat responds to noise and vulnerability
- Environmental danger increases over time

### Extraction

- Extraction only opens in the final phase
- Extraction capacity is limited
- Only extracted loot is secured

## Design Intent

- Early game cooperation
- Mid-game uncertainty
- Late-game betrayal and panic

## Mechanics To Delay

- Full weapon combat sandbox
- Complex crafting
- Large perk trees
- Multi-map queue
- Heavy narrative systems
