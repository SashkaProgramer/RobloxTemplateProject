# Match Phases

## Target Match Length

- `8-10 minutes` for MVP

## Phase 1: Preparation

- Players spawn in a safe starting carriage.
- The game establishes controls, movement, and first interaction without heavy tutorial text.
- Initial supplies are scarce but not contested.

## Phase 2: Traversal

- Players advance through wagons.
- Basic loot appears.
- Shared obstacles encourage cooperation.
- Threat level is manageable but rising.

## Phase 3: Escalation

- Lighting becomes less reliable.
- Valuable loot appears more often.
- Key resources become contested.
- Sabotage becomes more meaningful.

## Phase 4: Finale

- A major pressure spike begins.
- Extraction route or extraction terminal becomes active.
- Not all players can extract.
- Trust breaks down and endgame conflict peaks.

## Phase 5: Resolution

- Surviving extractors receive rewards.
- Dead or stranded players lose carried run loot.
- Match summary highlights extraction, loot value, and notable outcomes.
