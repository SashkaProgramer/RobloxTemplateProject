# Onboarding

## Goal

Teach the player the basic fantasy and first actions in under `30-60 seconds` without relying on heavy tutorial text.

## First Session Objectives

- Understand that this is a raid on a dangerous night train.
- Learn how to move, interact, and collect loot.
- Feel that cooperation is useful.
- Notice that danger and distrust will rise.

## First 30 Seconds

- Spawn the player in a dim but safe starting carriage.
- Show one obvious interactable object immediately.
- Let the player pick up a simple item quickly.
- Provide one clear forward path into the next carriage.

## What The Player Should Learn Naturally

- Loot can be picked up and carried.
- Doors and train progression matter.
- Staying near others feels safer early.
- The environment is hostile and unstable.

## Avoid In MVP

- Long text popups
- Multi-step written tutorial panels
- Complex pre-match systems before the first run
- Explaining every future mechanic up front

## Failure Conditions For Onboarding

- The player does not understand what the main objective is.
- The player misses the first interaction.
- The player does not feel pressure within the first minute.
- The player dies before learning the basic loop.
