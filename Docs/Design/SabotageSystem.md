# Sabotage System

## Purpose

Sabotage creates player-vs-player conflict without turning the game into a constant direct-combat brawler.

## MVP Sabotage Actions

### Lock Door

- Temporarily blocks a path
- Can isolate players from the group
- Strong near threat zones or extraction

### Cut Power

- Turns off light in a local area
- Increases confusion and danger
- Works well with PvE pressure

### Make Noise

- Attracts threats or accelerates danger
- Can be used to panic or split players

### Steal Key Resource

- Removes a resource the group needs
- Forces social suspicion and blame

## Design Principles

- Sabotage should be strong enough to matter but not so strong that it instantly ends the match with no counterplay.
- Sabotage should be more powerful later in the match than in the opening phase.
- Players should have enough information to become suspicious, but not perfect certainty every time.

## Counterplay

- Time-limited effects
- Recovery interactions
- Environmental cues
- Group coordination and observation

## Things To Avoid

- Unlimited spam actions
- Zero-risk sabotage
- Full anonymity with no readable consequence
- Sabotage that always beats cooperation
