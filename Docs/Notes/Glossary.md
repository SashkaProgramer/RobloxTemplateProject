# Glossary

## Raid

One full playable match from spawn to extraction or failure.

## Run Loot

Loot collected during the current match that is not secured until extraction succeeds.

## Secured Loot

Loot or value successfully kept after extraction.

## Rare Loot

High-value loot intended to alter player behavior and increase social tension.

## Key Resource

A required or strategically important item such as a fuse, battery, or access item.

## Sabotage

An intentional player action that disrupts, misleads, isolates, or endangers others without relying on direct combat-first PvP.

## Extraction

The final opportunity to escape the match and secure carried run loot.

## Extraction Capacity

The number of players who can successfully leave during the final evacuation.

## Finale

The last match phase where pressure spikes and extraction becomes decisive.

## Round State

The authoritative server-side state of the current match phase and its rules.
