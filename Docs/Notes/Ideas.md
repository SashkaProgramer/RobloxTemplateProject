# Ideas

## Atmosphere

- lights flicker before a threat appears
- certain wagons feel safer but contain less valuable loot
- distant metal sounds and train stress increase panic

## Sabotage

- players can trap others in a dark carriage briefly
- a sabotage action can attract a threat without revealing the saboteur immediately
- key resource theft forces social blame and suspicion

## Rare Loot

- a rare artifact gives strong visual feedback when found
- carrying rare loot could slightly increase danger or attention
- rare loot should be valuable enough to justify betrayal
