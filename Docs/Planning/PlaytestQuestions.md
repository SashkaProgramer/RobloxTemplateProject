# Playtest Questions

## First Session

- Did the player understand the objective within the first minute?
- Did the player find the first interaction naturally?
- Did the player feel tension early enough?

## Cooperation and Conflict

- Did players feel that staying together was useful early on?
- Did sabotage feel intentional and readable?
- Did conflict emerge naturally, or did the match collapse too early?

## Extraction

- Did players understand how extraction worked?
- Did limited extraction slots create tension?
- Did the final phase feel like a climax?

## Loot and Reward

- Did players care about finding rare loot?
- Did rare loot change player decisions?
- Did losing carried loot feel motivating or just frustrating?

## Atmosphere

- Did the train feel threatening and memorable?
- Did light, sound, and pressure create anxiety?
- Did the game feel grounded and atmospheric rather than random chaos?

## Interpretation Goal

Use these questions to detect confusion, pacing failures, weak tension, and systems that do not yet influence player behavior strongly enough.
