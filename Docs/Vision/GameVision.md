# Game Vision

## Working Title

`Night Train: Last Extraction`

## High Concept

A dark, atmospheric Roblox extraction-lite where players move through a night train, gather loot, survive escalating threats, and decide whether to cooperate or sabotage each other before limited evacuation slots disappear.

## Player Fantasy

- Survive a tense raid in a cursed moving train.
- Form temporary alliances under pressure.
- Betray, outplay, or outlast other players when extraction becomes scarce.
- Escape with valuable loot and visible proof of survival.

## Experience Goals

- Tense and readable in the first minute.
- Atmospheric rather than noisy or comedic.
- Cooperative at first, distrustful by the end.
- Replayable through player behavior, loot tension, and endgame drama.

## Tone

- Dark
- Chaotic in spikes
- Anxious
- Atmospheric
- Grounded and semi-realistic rather than cartoonish

## Target Outcome

- Build a strong MVP around one highly replayable match format.
- Reach a monetizable game loop without requiring large-world scope or heavy content production.
