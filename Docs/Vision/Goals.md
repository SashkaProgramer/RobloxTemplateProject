# Goals

## Primary Goal

- Build a shippable Roblox game with a strong enough gameplay loop and monetization base to pursue at least `11,000 EUR` in revenue over time.

## Product Goals

- Deliver a clear first-session hook in under 30 seconds.
- Make extraction outcomes emotionally memorable.
- Create strong social tension through limited evacuation and sabotage.
- Make rare loot desirable enough to influence player behavior.

## Development Goals

- Keep MVP scope small enough for iterative solo or small-team development.
- Prefer one polished match flow over multiple unfinished systems.
- Keep gameplay-significant logic server authoritative.

## Non-Goals For MVP

- Open-world survival
- Large weapon sandbox
- Deep crafting
- Story-heavy campaign
- Multiple maps at launch
