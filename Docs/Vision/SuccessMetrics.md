# Success Metrics

## Purpose

These metrics help judge whether the core loop is working before content scale becomes the focus.

## Early Product Metrics

- First-session completion rate
- Average match duration
- Extraction rate
- Rare loot pickup rate
- Requeue rate after match end

## Behavioral Signals

- How often players stay grouped early
- How often sabotage appears in mid or late match
- How often rare loot changes player behavior
- How often players reach the final extraction phase

## Warning Signs

- Players leave before the first meaningful interaction
- Players do not understand extraction
- Rare loot is ignored
- Sabotage is either never used or used immediately every match
- Most matches feel decided too early

## MVP Interpretation

- A lower extraction rate is acceptable if the match still feels fair and memorable.
- High rare loot interest is more important than perfect balance early.
- Strong rematch intent matters more than perfect monetization at MVP stage.
